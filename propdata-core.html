What's Included

Six data layers.
One subscription.

Each API hits a different government source. Together they give you a complete intelligence profile on any US address, ZIP code, or business in a single stack.

01
US ZIP Code Intelligence
Demographics, income, housing market, investment signals, flood risk, crime index, opportunity zone status, and urban/rural classification — all in one call per ZIP. Bulk lookup: 1–25 ZIPs per call. Built on the same infrastructure as PropData's 165M+ parcel database.
02
US Flood Zone Intelligence
Pass any address or lat/lng. Get the FEMA flood zone designation (AE, X, VE, etc.), risk level, SFHA flag, annual flood probability, Base Flood Elevation, and plain-English insurance advisory. Bulk: up to 25 addresses per call. Data from FEMA National Flood Hazard Layer.
03
US Crime Index
Composite crime score 0–100. Violent crime rate, property crime rate, homicide rate, national comparison percentages. 40+ major cities and all 50 states. FBI UCR 2022 data. Bulk: up to 25 City,ST pairs per call. Know the market before you buy.
04
US Census Intelligence
Full ACS profiles for any ZIP, county, or state. Population, income, education, housing, employment, commute, and race/ethnicity from the US Census Bureau's American Community Survey. Comparison matrix across up to 10 ZIPs for 13 metrics.
05
US Business Intelligence
EIN lookup via SEC EDGAR and the PropData learned database. Entity type, state of formation, NAICS sector, business name. Bulk: up to 50 EINs per call. Free EIN format validation with no key required. Self-improving: every lookup cached permanently.
06
US Address Validator & Standardizer
Pass any messy address string. Get USPS-standardized address, verified geocoordinates, county FIPS, confidence score, and property intelligence from 165M+ assessor records. Bulk: up to 50 addresses per call. Self-improving — every lookup written permanently to PropData.
