FAQ

Questions

Is there a free tier?
No. The demo key (psa_demo_propsports2026) exists for evaluation — MLB only, 20 req/hr globally capped across all callers — so you can verify the response shapes before you subscribe. It is not a production path. Basic at $49/mo is the entry point for production traffic.
How quickly do I get my API key?
Within 60 seconds of Stripe payment confirmation, your key is emailed to the address on your Stripe account. There is no manual review or setup call. Subscribe, copy the key, ship by Friday.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe customer portal at any time. No cancellation fees. Your key stays active until the end of the current billing period.
How is this different from SportRadar or SportsDataIO?
SportRadar pays the leagues for redistribution rights and charges $24,000+/year minimum with a 6-week contract review. SportsDataIO is in the same ballpark. PropSports wraps the leagues' free public APIs and adds a proprietary layer — owned Statcast + owned Poisson model — on top. Basic is $49/mo. No contract. Key in 60 seconds.
Where does the data come from?
Third-party pass-throughs: MLB Stats API, NHL Stats API, ESPN, stats.nba.com. Owned data: Baseball Savant Statcast (aggregated nightly into our Supabase), the PropBetEdge Poisson model (6x daily from owned Statcast), and normalized NBA boxscore math (TS% + USG% computed per player). No CoreLogic. No ATTOM. No SportRadar.
What is the PropBetEdge Poisson model?
Our proprietary Cloudflare Worker cron runs a Poisson distribution model against the latest Statcast data 6x daily (8AM, 10AM, 12PM, 2PM, 5PM, 8PM CT). For each batter-pitcher matchup we compute expected stat, OVER/UNDER probability, and American odds for HR, total bases, hits, strikeouts, RBI, and runs. Output is stored in our Supabase and served via /mlb/odds/model.
Can I use this for real-money sports wagering?
PropSports is a data and analytics platform — it does not facilitate, accept, or process any wager. The API is informational. For wagering purposes you must confirm every line on the sportsbook directly before placing a bet. Our Terms of Service require that you are 21+ and in a legal sports wagering jurisdiction.
Can I resell or redistribute the data?
Display within your application to your end users: yes. Resell raw API responses as a data product: not under Basic / Pro / Ultra. Yes under Mega / Enterprise with a signed Order Form and data license. Contact support@proptechusa.ai for enterprise licensing.
